Bumpkin Muffin Recipe

In this page you will learn how to make the most famous Brazilian Muffin at the interior of the SÃ£o Paulo state.


Ingredients (40 units)

    Muffin

  • 1/2 kg of Flaked Corn Flour
  • 1 & 1/2 L of Filtered Water
  • 2 Beef Bouillon Cubes
  • 4 tablespoons of Canola Oil
  • 2 tablespoons of White Cassava Flour

    Filling

  • 1/2 kg Lean Ground Beef
  • Choppeed Onions
  • Smashed Garlic
  • Chopped Parsley and Ciboulette
  • Salt and Pepper by the chef







Procedure

(Estimated preparing time: 30 minutes)

Filling

Put all filling ingredients together, with seasoning, and use the raw filling to prepare the muffing. This will avoid the ground beef gets too dry (not juicy).

Put aside and start to prepare the muffin dough.

Muffin Dough

  1. Put the Corn and Cassava Flour together and break the flakes completely (You can use a mixer if you want).
  2. Add the Canola oil and mix it until the flour appears like little wet. Then, reserve it for later.
  3. Boil the water with the beef cubes and let it chill like a soup (warm temperature).
  4. Now, pour the warm water (after boiling) into the flour in steps and mix it well until the mass becomes homogeneous and proper to get and shape it in hand.
  5. Separate a little portion and smash it into the palm of the hand.
  6. Put some ground beef filling into the middle of the muffin and close it around the filling, giving a shape of a conical muffin.
  7. After crafting the muffin, boil oil at a high temperature (you can test the temperature with a tiny ball of the muffin dough. If stays on the surface of the oil the temperature is good).
  8. Use a paper towel to absorb the excess oil.
  9. Serve the muffins hot! The cold muffin may not be as good as the warm one.

Enjoy!
